Welcome! Let’s enjoy art together!

I’m from the beautiful country of PANAMA!

I have been tattooing since 2007 in my hometown of Valdosta, Georgia. I travel all over the world doing conventions, and accomplishing some of the most awesome things that I could ever imagine, including winning awards and public recognition along the way while just doing what I love. I’m fascinated with art, and my goal is to create the most amazing tattoos for you: something unique that you will be proud for the rest of your life.
I don’t have a particular style; I enjoy doing all different styles: big or small projects in black and grey, color, realism, cover-ups, traditional, and neo-traditional concepts. Taking your ideas and translating them to the perfect tattoo for your needs is my specialty.
